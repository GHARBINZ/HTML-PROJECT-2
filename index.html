Carthage Flavors Menu

Appetizers

  • Tunisian Brik

    A thin, crispy pastry shell filled with a whole egg, tuna, parsley, and capers, deep-fried to golden perfection.

  • Slata Mechouia

    A smoky grilled salad made of charred peppers, tomatoes, and garlic, topped with tuna, boiled eggs, and olive oil.

  • Ojja with Merguez

    A spicy tomato and pepper stew with poached eggs and traditional lamb sausages (Merguez).

Main Courses

  • Lamb Couscous

    The national dish of Tunisia: fine semolina steamed over a savory broth with tender lamb, chickpeas, pumpkin, and carrots.

  • Lablabi

    A rich, garlicky chickpea soup served over crusty bread, flavored with cumin, harissa, and a soft-boiled egg.

  • Kamounia

    A thick, hearty beef and liver stew flavored heavily with cumin and served with fresh baguette.

Desserts

  • Baklawa Tunisienne

    Layers of paper-thin pastry filled with crushed almonds and hazelnuts, drenched in a honey and orange blossom syrup.

  • Assidat Zgougou

    A traditional cream made from Aleppo pine seeds, topped with a smooth custard layer and decorated with nuts.

  • Bambalouni

    Light and airy Tunisian donuts, deep-fried and dusted with granulated sugar, best enjoyed hot.
